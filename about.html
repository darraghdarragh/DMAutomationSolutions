Our Story

From websites to intelligent business systems

DM Automation Solutions was founded by Darragh Morrissey to help businesses build a stronger digital presence. What began as web design has evolved into a broader mission: creating smart systems that save time, reduce friction, and support growth.

Today, our work spans AI agents, automation workflows, premium websites, and connected business systems that make operations smoother and more scalable.
